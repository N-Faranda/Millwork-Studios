What it does

Built for the work that comes back tomorrow.

i.

Furrows, not folders

Group work by the rhythm it actually has — by season, by client, by quiet recurring routine. Plough doesn’t pretend everything is a project.

ii.

One list, every day

Your team opens to a single morning list. What’s due, what’s overdue, what’s growing. No dashboards, no widgets.

iii.

Quiet handovers

When someone’s away, their furrow keeps moving. Reassignments leave a paper trail; nobody has to ask twice.

iv.

The miller’s ledger

Every closed task becomes a line in your studio’s ledger — a calm, searchable record of what got done and by whom.

v.

Offline, by default

Plough works in the back of a bakery, on a building site, on the train. Sync resumes when you’re back in range.

vi.

Your data, your mill

Export everything as Markdown or CSV at any time. Self-hosting available on the Atelier plan.
